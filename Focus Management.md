## Q1: What is Deep Work?  
Deep work is focused, undistracted, and intense work that allows you to produce high quality results in less time. 
It involves minimizing distractions to engage in cognitively demanding tasks.

## Q2: According to author how to do deep work properly in a few points?  
- Schedule deep work sessions in advance.  
- Eliminate distractions totally during these sessions.  
- Work in a place that is free from interruptions.

## Q3: How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?  
- Set strict time blocks for deep work.  
- Create a distraction-free environment.  
- Make deep work a priority and avoid multitasking.

## Q4: What are the dangers of social media, in brief?  
- Social media can cause distraction, reduce focus, and waste time.  
- It can also promote comparison and negatively affect mental health.  
- Overuse can lead to a decline in productivity.
