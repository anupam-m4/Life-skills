## 1.What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

Sexual harassment includes any unwelcome sexual comments jokes , gestures or messages that make someone uncomfortable.
It can also involve unwanted physical contact repeated or asking for favors in exchange for benefits.
Even staring spreading rumors or creating an unsafe hostile environment counts as harassment.

## 2.What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

If I face it i would clearly tell the person to stop and make it known that the behaviour is not acceptable.
I would keep a record of what happened and report it to my superior or the Internal Committee.
In case I witness such behaviour I would first check in with the person affected and offer my support.

## 4.How to handle cases of harassment?

Take every complaint seriously and listen without judging the person who reports it.
Follow the proper reporting process and involve the Internal Committee as per policy.
Maintain confidentiality and ensure fair investigation and action.

## 5.How to behave appropriately?

Always respect personal boundaries and maintain professional conduct at work.
Think before speaking or acting when it involves sensitive topics.
Create a safe respectful environment where everyone feels comfortable and valued.
