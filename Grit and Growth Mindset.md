## 1. Grit

Grit means staying committed to a goal for a long time, even when it gets difficult.
The video shows that steady effort and not giving up matter more than natural talent.

---
## 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset

A growth mindset is believing you can improve your skills through practice.
Instead of fearing failure, you see it as a chance to learn and get better.

---
## 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control

Internal Locus of Control means believing your choices shape your results.
The main idea is to focus on what you can control instead of blaming situations or others.

---

## 4. How to Build a Growth Mindset

* Try difficult tasks even if you might fail.
* Learn from mistakes.
* Accept feedback positively.
* Keep improving step by step.
---

## 5. My Ideas to Take Action and Build Growth Mindset

* Code regularly and practice new concepts.
* Reflect on errors and fix them patiently.
* Take feedback seriously.
* Stay consistent and disciplined.
